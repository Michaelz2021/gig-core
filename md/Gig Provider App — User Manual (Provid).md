# Gig Provider App — User Manual (Provider)

This manual describes how to use the **Gig Provider App** (GIG-HANAP PRO) as a **service provider**: signing in, browsing work, managing quotes and jobs, messaging, profile, wallet, and verification.

---

## 1. Getting started

### 1.1 Opening the app

- Open the **Gig Provider** app on your device.
- You will see either the **login** screen (if not signed in) or the **main home** screen (if already logged in).

### 1.2 Creating an account

1. On the login screen, tap **Register** (or equivalent sign-up link).
2. Enter your details as required (e.g. email or phone, password, name).
3. Complete any **OTP (one-time password)** step if shown (email or phone).
4. After verification, you can log in and use the app as a provider.

### 1.3 Logging in

- **Email login**: Enter email and password. You may have a “Remember me” option.
- **Phone login**: Select country code (e.g. Philippines +63), enter phone number and password.
- Tap **Login**. If OTP is required, enter the code sent to your email or phone.

### 1.4 Forgot password

- On the login screen, tap **Forgot password** (or similar).
- Follow the steps to reset your password (usually via email or phone).

---

## 2. Main navigation (bottom bar)

After login, the main screen has a **bottom navigation bar** with five tabs:

| Tab        | Icon   | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Home**  | Home   | Marketplace dashboard: categories, recommended pros, and quick actions. |
| **Search**| Search | Search for projects or categories. |
| **My Jobs** | Work | Your quotes, contracts, in-progress jobs, and completed jobs. |
| **Messages** | Chat | Conversations with clients. |
| **Profile** | Person | Your provider profile, stats, wallet, portfolio, and verification. |

Tap a tab to switch between these sections.

---

## 3. Home tab

### 3.1 App bar

- **Title**: “GIG-HANAP PRO”.
- **Notifications** (bell icon): Tap to open the notification list. A badge shows the count of unread notifications.
- **Settings/Logout** (gear icon): Tap to log out. A confirmation dialog appears before signing out.

### 3.2 Marketplace dashboard

- **Categories**: Service categories are shown (with icons when available). Tap a category to see related projects or services.
- **Recommended Pros**: A list of recommended providers; tap to view their profile or services.
- **Quick actions** (if shown): Shortcuts such as Post Project, My Projects, Messages, My Profile.

Use this tab to explore categories and opportunities before going to **My Jobs** or **Search**.

---

## 4. Search tab

- Use the search bar to find **projects**, **categories**, or **providers**.
- You can open **filters** (e.g. category, location) to narrow results.
- Results may include projects you can bid on; tap an item to view details and submit a quote from **My Jobs** or the detail screen.

---

## 5. My Jobs tab

This tab is the main place to manage your work as a provider. It has **four sub-tabs**:

### 5.1 Quotes

- Lists your **submitted quotes/bids** (“My Quote Submissions”).
- Each card shows project title, auction number, status, and actions.
- Tap a quote to view details or see the related project/auction.
- Pull down to **refresh** the list.

### 5.2 Contract

- Shows jobs that are **contracted** (client accepted your quote).
- Tap a contract to open **booking/contract details**, accept, or manage the job.

### 5.3 In Progress

- Lists jobs currently **in progress**.
- You can add work reports, update status, or communicate with the client from the job detail screen.

### 5.4 Completed

- Lists **completed** jobs.
- Useful for reviewing past work and ratings.

### 5.5 Submitting a quote (bid)

- From a project/auction (e.g. from Search or a category), open the project and tap to **Submit quote** (or “Bid”).
- On the **Submit Quote** screen you can enter:
  - **Price** (e.g. labor, materials breakdown).
  - **Schedule**: estimated hours, start date, start time.
  - **Proposal**: title, description, methodology.
  - **Materials**: included/excluded.
  - **Warranty**: days and description.
  - **Portfolio images** and **competitive advantages** (if supported).
- Submit the quote. It will appear under **My Jobs → Quotes** and move to **Contract** when the client accepts.

### 5.6 Booking / job details

- From **My Jobs** (any sub-tab), tap a card to open **Booking detail**.
- You can:
  - View full project and client info.
  - Accept or decline (for new requests).
  - Add **work reports** (e.g. photos, notes).
  - See contract and payment/escrow info if applicable.

---

## 6. Quick order (instant booking)

- When a client sends an **instant booking request**, you may receive a **push notification** (“New Instant Booking Request”).
- Tapping it opens the **Quick Order Service** screen with:
  - Booking ID, service category, time slot, location.
  - Your **matching listings** (services you offer in that category).
- Select one or more listings and tap **Accept Order** to accept the instant booking.
- If you do not have matching listings, add or edit services in **Profile → Quick Order** (or equivalent) so you can accept such requests next time.

---

## 7. Messages tab

- Shows your **chat conversations** with clients.
- Tap a conversation to open the **Chat** screen and send/receive messages.
- Use this for job-related communication (e.g. after a quote is accepted or a contract is active).

---

## 8. Profile tab

The **Profile** screen shows your public provider profile and quick access to wallet, portfolio, and verification.

### 8.1 Profile header

- **Cover**: Teal header with your **profile picture** (avatar) overlapping the header and content.
- **Name** and subtitle “Professional Service Provider”.

### 8.2 Performance metrics

- **Trust**: Trust score (e.g. from platform).
- **Rate**: Your hourly or service rate (e.g. ₱).
- **Jobs done**: Number of completed projects.
- **Avg. rating**: Average rating from clients.
- **Earnings**: Total earnings (if shown).

### 8.3 Bookings stats

- **In Progress**: Number of jobs in progress.
- **Total Bids**: Total quote submissions.
- **Completed**: Completed jobs.
- **Active**: Active contracts/jobs.

These numbers are loaded when you open the Profile tab.

### 8.4 Edit Profile button

- Tap **Edit Profile** to open the **Edit Profile** screen.
- You can update:
  - **Name** (first, last).
  - **Phone**, **gender**.
  - **Profile photo** (take or choose from gallery).
  - **Service categories** (multi-level category selection).
- Save to update your profile.

### 8.5 Wallet section (on Profile)

Three shortcuts:

- **Reward Credits**: Opens the **Credit/Wallet** screen (earn or use reward credits).
- **Pay Out**: Opens **Payout/Withdraw** to withdraw earnings.
- **Pay Out Account**: Opens **Register Bank Account** to add or manage the bank account used for payouts.

### 8.6 Address information

- If you have set address, city, province, or zip code, they are shown here.
- Edit these in **Edit Profile** if the app supports it.

### 8.7 Portfolio

- **Portfolio** shows your uploaded **portfolio photos** (work samples).
- Tap **Add** (when empty) or **Edit** to add or change photos and captions.
- Portfolio is used to showcase your work to clients and in quotes.

### 8.8 Quick Order (listings)

- **Quick Order** lists the services you offer for **instant booking** (e.g. “Quick Order Offer”).
- You can add or edit listings so they appear when a client sends an instant booking in a given category.
- Tap a listing to view or edit details.

### 8.9 Advertise

- **Advertise** (or “My Ads”) shows your **provider ads** (active/expired).
- You can create or manage ads to promote your services.

### 8.10 Reviews

- **Reviews** shows ratings and comments from clients who have worked with you.
- Reviews are loaded from the server for your profile.

### 8.11 Verification

- **Verification** summarizes your verification status (e.g. ID, certificates, portfolio).
- Tap to open the **Verification & Trust Score** hub (see Section 9).

### 8.12 Trust score

- **Trust Score** (or analysis) may be shown at the bottom of the profile, explaining how your trust score is calculated or how to improve it.

---

## 9. Verification & Trust Score

- From **Profile → Verification** (or the verification hub entry), you open the **Verification & Trust Score** screen.

### 9.1 Verification progress

- Steps typically include:
  - **Email** verified
  - **Phone** verified
  - **ID** verified
  - **Certificate** (if applicable)
  - **Portfolio** (photos uploaded)

Completion is shown with a progress strip or similar.

### 9.2 Completing verification

- **Email/Phone**: Use the app or link sent to your email/phone to verify.
- **ID verification**: Follow the in-app flow (e.g. upload ID document, selfie/liveness if required).
- **Certificate**: Upload or link certificates if the app supports it.
- **Portfolio**: If you have no portfolio yet, you are guided to **Portfolio submission**; if you already have one, you are taken to **Edit portfolio** to add or change photos.

### 9.3 Trust score

- The same screen or a **Trust Score** screen may explain how your trust score is computed and how to improve it (e.g. complete verification, complete jobs, get good reviews).

---

## 10. Wallet, credits & payout

### 10.1 Reward credits (Credit wallet)

- From **Profile → Reward Credits** you open the **Credit/Wallet** screen.
- You can:
  - View **credit balance**.
  - **Purchase** credits or **use** credits (e.g. for promotions or fees).
  - **Withdraw** credits to your payout account if supported.

### 10.2 Pay out (withdraw)

- From **Profile → Pay Out** you open the **Payout/Withdraw** screen.
- Select amount and confirm to send earnings to your **Pay Out Account** (bank account).
- You may need to complete **Pay Out Account** setup first.

### 10.3 Pay out account (bank account)

- From **Profile → Pay Out Account** you open **Register Bank Account** (or bank account management).
- Add or edit your **bank account** details so the platform can send your payouts.
- Keep details accurate to avoid failed or delayed withdrawals.

### 10.4 Transaction history

- From the wallet or payout screens, there is usually a way to open **Transaction history** to see past top-ups, withdrawals, and job-related payments.

---

## 11. Other features

### 11.1 Notifications

- Tap the **bell** icon in the app bar to open the notification list.
- Notifications can include:
  - New instant booking requests
  - New messages
  - Quote accepted / contract updates
  - Job reminders or status changes
- Tap a notification to open the related screen (e.g. Quick Order, Chat, Booking detail).

### 11.2 Escrow (if applicable)

- For jobs that use **escrow**, you may have an **Escrow** entry (e.g. from wallet or profile).
- Open **Escrow list** to see escrow transactions; open one for **Escrow details** (amount, status, release conditions).

### 11.3 Contracts

- From **My Jobs → Contract** or from a booking, you can open **Contract** to view or sign the service agreement.

### 11.4 Work reports

- For **in-progress** jobs, open the **Booking detail** and use **Add work report** (or similar) to upload photos or notes for the client.
- This helps document progress and can support release of escrow or completion.

---

## 12. Logging out

- Tap the **settings/gear** icon in the app bar on the main (Home) screen.
- Confirm **Log out** in the dialog.
- You are returned to the **Login** screen. Your account remains; you can log in again anytime.

---

## 13. Summary checklist (provider flow)

1. **Register / Login** → Complete OTP if asked.
2. **Profile** → Edit profile, add **portfolio**, set **Quick Order** listings, complete **Verification**.
3. **Wallet** → Set **Pay Out Account** (bank) so you can withdraw later.
4. **Home / Search** → Browse categories and projects.
5. **My Jobs → Quotes** → Submit quotes on projects; track them under Quotes, then Contract when accepted.
6. **Quick Order** → When you get an instant booking notification, open it and **Accept** with the right listing.
7. **My Jobs → Contract / In Progress** → Accept contract, do the job, add **work reports**.
8. **Messages** → Communicate with clients.
9. **My Jobs → Completed** → Review completed jobs and ratings.
10. **Profile → Pay Out** → Withdraw earnings to your bank account.
