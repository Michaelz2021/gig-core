## 1. Getting Started

- **Log in** with your account (email/phone and password, or your configured auth method).
- After login you see the **Home** tab with the main dashboard.
- The bottom bar has five tabs: **Home**, **Post**, **My Order**, **Messages**, and **Profile**.

---

## 2. Main Navigation

| Tab        | Icon   | Purpose |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Home**  | House  | Browse categories, Order Now, top providers, promotions, and latest reviews. |
| **Post**  | Plus   | Create a new project (request for quotes from providers). |
| **My Order** | Briefcase | View and manage your orders: Quick Order list, Awaiting Bids, Contracted, In Progress, Completed. |
| **Messages** | Chat | Open your chat list and talk to providers. |
| **Profile** | Person | View and edit your profile, Reward Credits, My Favorite Providers, and statistics. |

- Tapping **Post** opens the **Post New Project** flow directly.
- The top bar shows the app name **GIG-HANAP KO**, plus **Notifications** (bell) and **Settings** (gear). Tapping the gear can open a **Log out** confirmation.

---

## 3. Home

On **Home** you see (in order):

1. **Order Now**  
   - A large banner/card.  
   - Tap it to go to **Order Now** (Quick Order) and request instant services.

2. **Main service categories**  
   - Four category icons (e.g. Home, IT, Design, etc.).  
   - Tap a category to browse **Recommended Pro** providers in that category.

3. **Top Tier Service Workers**  
   - Ranked list of recommended providers.  
   - Tap a provider card to open their **Provider Details** screen.

4. **Service promotions**  
   - Promotional banners.  
   - Tap to see promotion details if available.

5. **New Service Providers**  
   - List of new providers.  
   - Tap a card to open **Provider Details**.

6. **Latest Reviews**  
   - Recent reviews from the platform.

- You can also use the **search bar** (when visible) to search for services or professionals.  
- **Category detail** screens show **Recommended Pro** cards; each card can have a **heart (favorite)** icon to add or remove the provider from **My Favorite Providers** (see [Section 9](#9-provider-profile--favorites)).

---

## 4. Post (Request a Project)

- Tap the **Post** tab to start **Post New Project**.

**Typical flow:**

1. **Step 1 — Project details**  
   - Enter **service title**, **description**, **location**, **budget** (min/max), **requirements**, and **deadline** (if needed).  
   - Move to the next step.

2. **Step 2 — Review & submit**  
   - Review the information.  
   - Tap **Post** (or equivalent) to publish the project.

- After posting, the project appears under **My Order** in the **Awaiting Bids** tab so you can see and compare bids from providers.

- Some flows may include an **AI assistant** or **RFQ preview**; follow the on-screen steps to complete the request.

---

## 5. My Order

**My Order** has five tabs:

| Tab               | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **Quick Order**   | List of your **Order Now** (instant) orders. Tap an item to see **Detail** (e.g. in a bottom sheet). |
| **Awaiting Bids** | Projects you posted that are waiting for provider bids. Tap a project to see and compare bids. |
| **Contracted**    | Orders that are contracted but not yet in progress. You can open contract/signing or payment steps from here. |
| **In Progress**   | Orders currently being worked on. |
| **Completed**     | Finished orders. You can open **Write Review**, **Service Reward**, or **Chat** from the booking card. |

**From a booking/order card you can:**

- Open **Detail** (e.g. booking details, invoice).  
- **Write Review** — rate and review the provider after completion.  
- **Service Reward** — use or earn reward credits linked to the booking.  
- **Chat** — start or continue a conversation with the provider.  
- **Payment** — pay for the order (e.g. from Contracted or when the app shows a payment step).

---

## 6. Messages

- Tap **Messages** to open your **chat list**.
- Each conversation shows the other party (usually a provider).
- Tap a conversation to open the **chat screen** and send/receive messages.
- Notifications (bell icon on Home) may show new message alerts.

---

## 7. Profile

On **Profile** you see:

1. **Profile card**  
   - Phone number, **CONSUMER** badge, and a **Detail Profile** button.  
   - Tap **Detail Profile** to go to the **Edit Profile** screen and update your details.

2. **Reward Credits**  
   - Row: “View credits, purchase, history” with an arrow.  
   - Tap to open the **Wallet** / Reward Credits screen (see [Section 11](#11-reward-credits--wallet)).

3. **My Favorite Providers**  
   - List of providers you have **favorited** (heart icon).  
   - Each row shows the **provider name or company name** (not the ID).  
   - Tap a row to open that provider’s **Provider Details** screen.  
   - If the list is empty, the message says: “No favorite providers yet. Add from provider profiles.”

4. **Statistics**  
   - Cards for **Posted**, **Purchased**, **Active**, and **Spent** (e.g. count of projects and amount spent).

---

## 8. Order Now (Quick Order)

- **From Home:** Tap the **Order Now** banner.  
- **From My Order:** Use the **Quick Order** tab to see existing quick orders; you can also reach the Order Now flow from the app’s main entry (e.g. Home).

**Order Now flow (summary):**

1. **Choose service type**  
   - Pick from a grid of **instant services** (e.g. HOME SERVICES and others). Icons may come from the server (e.g. category icons).

2. **Set location & time**  
   - Choose **Home** or **On Site** (location type).  
   - Enter or select **location**, **date**, and **time**.

3. **Select provider & options**  
   - See responding providers and their offerings (e.g. variants/add-ons, price).  
   - Select provider, variant, add-ons, and quantity as needed.

4. **Confirm & pay**  
   - Review the order and tap **Confirm Order** (or similar).  
   - You may be taken to a **payment** screen to complete the transaction.

- Completed **Order Now** orders appear under **My Order → Quick Order** and can be opened for **Detail**.

## 9. Provider Profile & Favorites

- From **Home** (Top Tier, New Service Providers, category detail, or search results), or from **My Order** (e.g. bid cards), tap a **provider card** to open **Provider Details**.

**On Provider Details:**

- You see the provider’s info: name, photo, rating, reviews, skills, bio, etc.  
- **Heart icon (♡)** in the app bar:  
  - **Outline heart** — not in favorites. Tap to **add to favorites**.  
  - **Filled red heart** — in favorites. Tap to **remove from favorites**.  
- **Share icon** in the app bar:  
  - Tap to **recommend** the provider.  
  - A bottom sheet may ask for an optional short **note** (e.g. “Very professional, on time!”).  
  - Submit to add a **Recommend** for this provider.

**On provider cards (e.g. category detail or bid list):**

- A **heart icon** on the card lets you add or remove that provider from **My Favorite Providers** without opening the full profile.

**Favorites list:**

- All favorited providers appear in **Profile → My Favorite Providers** with their **name or company name**. Tap to open **Provider Details**.

## 10. Reviews, Like & Recommend

**Write Review (after a completed order):**

- From **My Order → Completed**, open a booking and tap **Write Review**.  
- Enter **star ratings** (e.g. Overall, Quality, Communication, Punctuality, Professionalism) and an optional **comment**.  
- **“I’d work with this provider again”** — turn this **on** to send a **Like** for the provider (only shown when the booking has a provider).  
- **“Share this Provider”** — tap to **Recommend** the provider; you can add an optional note and link the recommendation to the booking.  
- Submit the review. If you turned on “I’d work with this provider again,” a **Like** is recorded; if you used “Share this Provider,” a **Recommend** is recorded.

**Like & Recommend (summary):**

- **Like** — “I’d work with this provider again” (e.g. on the review screen after service).  
- **Recommend** — “Share this Provider” with an optional note (from review screen or from Provider Details Share button).  
- **Favorite** — heart icon on provider cards or Provider Details; saved in **My Favorite Providers** on Profile.

---

## 11. Reward Credits & Wallet

- From **Profile**, tap **Reward Credits** (“View credits, purchase, history”).  
- You are taken to the **Wallet** / Reward Credits screen where you can:  
  - View your **credit balance**.  
  - **Purchase** or **top up** credits.  
  - View **history** of credit usage.  
- **Service Reward** (from a completed booking in **My Order → Completed**) may let you earn or spend credits linked to that booking.

---

## 12. Settings & Logout

- On the main **Home** screen, tap the **gear icon** in the app bar.  
- A **Confirm** dialog may ask: “Do you want to log out?”  
- Choose **Log out** to sign out, or **Cancel** to stay.  
- After logout you are returned to the **Login** screen.
